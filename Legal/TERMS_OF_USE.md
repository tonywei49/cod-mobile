## 1. Description of the App

Gogodex is an iOS companion app for controlling a Codex runtime on your paired computer. The App can connect directly or through hosted relay infrastructure. Core features may include chat, git actions, photo attachments, voice transcription, pairing, trusted reconnect, and subscription-gated functionality.

Most coding, repository, and workspace operations run on your paired computer, not on the hosted relay.
